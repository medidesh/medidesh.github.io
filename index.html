How It Works

1. Purchase

Record purchases by supplier, invoice, and batch.

2. Stock

Batches updated with strips/units, prices, expiry.

3. Sell

POS subtracts from FIFO batches automatically.

4. Requests

Customers request; pharmacies accept or counter.

5. Alerts

Low stock & expiry reminders with actions.

6. Analytics

Sales, margins, top SKUs, returns.
