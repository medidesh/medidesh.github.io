Medidesh Pricing Model

Medidesh offers three simple plans designed for pharmacies of different sizes. The plans are named after major rivers of Bangladesh.

---

Podma Plan
Price: Free

Designed for small pharmacies that want to start using digital management.

Included Features

• 1 pharmacy
• 1 user (Owner)
• 1 device
• Basic inventory management
• Basic sales and purchase tracking
• Mobile app access
• Advertisements included

This plan allows pharmacies to experience Medidesh before upgrading.

---

Meghna Plan
Price: 399 BDT per month

Best for growing pharmacies that need full management tools.

Included Features

• 1 pharmacy
• Up to 3 users (Owner, Manager, Staff)
• Up to 2 devices
• Full inventory management
• Full accounting management
• Expiry reminders
• Low stock alerts
• Stock out alerts
• Report downloads (PDF, Excel, CSV)
• Thermal printing support
• Quick customer support
• Advertisements included

---

Jamuna Plan
Price: 899 BDT per month

Designed for pharmacies that want advanced features and priority services.

Included Features

• 1 pharmacy
• Up to 5 users (Owner, Manager, Staff)
• Up to 2 devices
• Full inventory management
• Full accounting management
• Expiry reminders and stock alerts
• Advanced reports and analytics
• Report downloads (PDF, Excel, CSV)
• Thermal printing support
• Priority customer support
• No advertisements
• Customer medicine demand search feature
